# Travel Planner - User Guide ✈️

Welcome to your Travel Planner! This guide explains how to use the dashboard specifically from an end-user's perspective to get the most out of planning your adventures.

## Accessing the App
Start by opening the application link in any modern web browser on your phone, tablet, or computer:
👉 **[Open Travel Planner Dashboard](https://travelplanner-black.vercel.app/)**

---

## 1. Planning Your First Trip
The core of the app revolves around "Trips". Let's create one:
1. From the main Dashboard, click the **Plan New Trip** button.
2. Enter your primary destination (e.g., "Paris, France"). 
3. *Magic feature:* Click the **Generate Image** button below the image placeholder. The app will automatically search the internet and fetch a beautiful cover photo of your destination!
4. Fill in the rest of the details: Trip Name, Dates of travel, and your Total estimated Bugdet in INR (₹).
5. Click **Create Trip**.
6. You will instantly be redirected to your `Trips` library, where your new adventure is saved!

---

## 2. Tracking Your Budget
Once you have a trip planned, you can start logging expenses as you travel or book items in advance to ensure you don't overspend.
1. Navigate to the **Budget** tab using the bottom navigation bar (or sidebar on desktop).
2. At the top of the screen, you will see a dropdown menu. Select the specific trip you want to track expenses for (or select "All Trips" to see your year-to-date spending).
3. Click the **Add Expense** button.
4. Enter a description (e.g., "Eiffel Tower Tickets"), the amount spent (e.g., "2500"), and choose a category from the dropdown (e.g., "Activities").
5. Click **Save**.
6. **Watch the magic:** The visual progress bar and total spent indicators will instantly update to reflect your new budget!

---

## 3. The Document Vault
Never lose track of a reservation or ID again.
1. Navigate to the **Documents** tab.
2. Click **Add Document**.
3. Fill out the identifying info: Name (e.g., "Boarding Pass"), Number, and Expiry Date if applicable (useful for passports!).
4. Click the upload area to select a PDF, image, or screenshot of your document directly from your device.
5. Click **Save Document**. 
6. When you are traveling and need to show the document, simply open the app, find it in the list, and click the **Download** icon to instantly view the saved image/PDF.

---

## 4. Understanding Your Dashboard
Your main home screen provides a high-level overview of everything you have planned.
*   **Live Weather:** If you have an upcoming trip scheduled, the dashboard will automatically pinpoint the destination's latitude and longitude and display a live weather widget showing the current temperature and conditions at your travel spot.
*   **Travel Insights:** See fun analytics like your most visited location, your average spending per transaction, and how many trips you are taking this year.
*   **Upcoming Adventures:** A snapshot view of the very next trips you have on the calendar, acting as a countdown!

*Safe travels!* 🌍
